Quick Guide

This quick guide will help you understand the game basics. Your main goal is to put the falling waste on the correct recycling bin, don't miss and score as many points as you can!

Basic Rules

These basic rules will help you score a lot of points.

Recycle!

  • Watch out! Recycling bins fill up!
  • Use the keys [UP] or [W] to empty a recycling bin!
  • You will gain more time when you recycle!
  • Do not put more waste on a full recycling bin!!! You will lose points!

Recycler

  • You have one minute [1:00] to recycle as much as you can.
  • Waste falls one by one put in the correct recycling bin
  • You can only play 20 times

Visual Aid

  • Use the visual aid colors if you need some help.
  • The next waste to drop is the one to the right.

Combo

  • Don't miss to increase your combo!
  • Max combo is 5.

Game Controls

Use the arrows of your keyboard to play, you can also use the alternative controls
(Play on a larger screen, it will be easier).


    UP / W
    It will empty your recycling bin, and you will gain extra time.
    LEFT / A
    Move the falling waste one column to the left.
    RIGHT / D
    Move the falling waste one column to the right.
    DOWN / S
    The falling waste will increase its speed. Make sure you are on the correct lane.
